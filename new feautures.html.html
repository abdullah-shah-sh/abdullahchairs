Low back office Chairs

1.Compact Design: Low back chairs typically have a smaller and more compact backrest, making them ideal for spaces where a more minimalistic or less bulky chair is needed.

2.Support for Lower Spine: While they don't offer full lumbar support like high-back chairs

3.Adjustability: Many low back chairs come with features like adjustable seat height.

4.Lightweight and Portable: Due to their smaller size, low back chairs are often lightweight and easier to move or reposition.
